Founder Statement

Dear Stakeholder, Welcome to SusRecomm. We are a Kenyan Incorporated and 100% youth-owned company endeavoring to deliver a sustainable future through innovative solutions to households and communities. We dream of a sustainable world where we are prepared even for the once in a million years calamity occurrences. We continuously and iteratively engage our young minds -to blend social, financial, environmental, and technological tools at our disposal in tackling infrastructural challenges around us.
